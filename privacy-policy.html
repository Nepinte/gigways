Privacy Policy - GigWays

Effective Date: [03/11/2025]

1. Introduction

Welcome to GigWays. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, state location, and profile details.
  • Usage Data: Work schedules, community interactions, and engagement within the app.
  • Device & Location Data: Driving activity detection, GPS-based location for safety features, and notification tracking.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and app performance analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and enhance our services, including scheduling, notifications, and community engagement.
  • To detect driving activity and ensure safe usage of the application.
  • To analyze app performance and optimize user experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Sharing & Security

  • We do not sell or trade your personal information.
  • We may share anonymized data for analytics and service improvements.
  • We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.
  • Your account data is accessible only by authorized personnel for maintenance and support.

5. Notifications & Tracking

  • Users receive essential notifications regarding work schedules, rest breaks, and safety alerts.
  • Driving detection is used solely for ensuring user safety and improving app features.
  • Location tracking is optional and can be disabled by users within settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations. Users may request data deletion or modification at any time.

7. User Rights & Control

  • Users can access, update, or delete their personal information within their account settings.
  • Users have the right to opt out of certain data processing activities.
  • Requests for data modification or removal can be sent to our support team.

8. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as analytics providers and payment processors) to enhance our platform. These services have their own privacy policies governing data usage.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or app updates.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Gigwaysofficial@gmail.com.
