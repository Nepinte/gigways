Privacy Policy - GigWays

Effective Date: March 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to GigWays. At GigWays LLC (“GigWays,” “we,” “our,” or “us”), safeguarding your privacy is a fundamental commitment. This Privacy Policy delineates how we collect, utilize, store, and protect your personal and usage data when you engage with our services, including the GigWays mobile application (“the App”).

GigWays is meticulously crafted to empower independent gig economy professionals—including rideshare drivers, delivery couriers, truckers, and other service providers—by offering robust tools to efficiently manage schedules, monitor earnings and expenses, and foster connections within a supportive community. Additionally, we provide safety-oriented features and driving activity tools, alongside options for collective voice coordination.

This Privacy Policy adheres strictly to applicable data protection laws and industry standards, encompassing the rigorous requirements of both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. By downloading, accessing, or utilizing the GigWays app, you acknowledge and consent to the data practices detailed herein. We encourage you to thoroughly review this policy to fully understand how your information is responsibly handled and protected.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our core services. The types of information we collect are listed below.

  • a. Personal Information: This includes your full name, email address, phone number, and state of residence. We may also collect any user-generated profile details you provide, which helps us personalize your experience.
  • b. Activity & Usage Data: We collect information regarding your work schedules, hours worked, earnings, expenses, and any interactions you make with the app. This data helps us deliver our services and improve the overall user experience.
  • c. Location & Motion Data (Optional and Consent-Based): We may use location services (e.g., GPS) to provide certain features like rest alerts, route optimization, and optional strike zone tracking. Driving activity detection is used to monitor your driving behavior and send you reminders to take rest breaks. These features are optional and activated only with your explicit consent.
  • d. Device & Technical Data: We collect technical information about your device, such as device type, operating system, IP address, and app usage logs. This information helps us resolve technical issues, enhance app performance, and ensure a smooth user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to provide you with a seamless, personalized experience within the GigWays app. Specifically, we use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and optimize features such as scheduling, notifications, and earnings tracking to assist you in managing your gig work more effectively.
  • To support optional safety features, including rest reminders and driving detection (only with your consent), ensuring you take appropriate breaks during driving or working.
  • To help you generate and manage work documentation, such as mileage summaries and time logs, for the purpose of assisting with tax filing and record keeping.
  • Analyze app usage to improve functionality, design, and overall performance based on real user patterns.
  • Offer community features like job boards and optional involvement in voice and awareness activities. Participation in such activities is fully voluntary and at your discretion.

We do not use your information for marketing or share it with third-party advertisers. Your involvement in any community initiatives, including voice-related activities, is completely optional—GigWays neither encourages nor requires participation.

4. Data Sharing & Security

At GigWays, we take your privacy seriously. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third-party advertisers. However, we may share anonymized, aggregated data with trusted partners for analytical and operational purposes to enhance app performance and functionality. This data cannot be used to personally identify you.

We employ state-of-the-art encryption to protect your personal data both during transmission and while stored. Your data is secure and only accessible to authorized personnel for the purpose of maintaining and improving our services.

5. Location & Tracking (Apple & Google Compliant)

GigWays utilizes location and motion tracking features to enhance the app's functionality. These features are enabled only with your explicit consent and are used to provide:

  • Safety alerts and rest reminders while driving or working.
  • Work documentation features, such as mileage tracking and time logs.
  • Optional strike zone awareness features for users participating in voluntary strike activities.

These location and motion tracking features are entirely optional and disabled by default. You can enable them at any time in the app settings, and you retain the ability to disable them at your discretion. You may also revoke access to these features in your device's privacy settings at any time.

6. Notifications

GigWays may send notifications to keep you informed about your work schedule, breaks, and earnings. We also send reminders for rest periods and driving activity alerts if these features are enabled.

You can manage or disable these notifications at any time through your device's notification settings. Notifications will not be sent without your explicit consent.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal and usage data only for as long as necessary to provide our services, support user requests, and comply with applicable legal obligations. If you choose to delete your account or request data deletion, we will retain only the minimal data required to fulfill legal obligations, such as tax or regulatory purposes.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also withdraw your consent for data collection at any time, though this may limit your ability to use certain features of the app.

If you wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at gigwaysofficial@gmail.com.

GigWays reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy as necessary to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email. The most recent version of this policy will be available on our website and within the app.
