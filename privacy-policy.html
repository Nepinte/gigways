1. Introduction

Welcome to Gigways LLC (“Gigways,” “we,” “our,” or “us”). Your privacy and the security of your personal information are of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy has been carefully crafted to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of how we collect, utilize, safeguard, and store the personal and usage data you provide when you access or interact with our mobile application (“the App”) and any related services.

Gigways is designed specifically to empower independent contractors and professionals operating within the gig economy—this includes rideshare drivers, delivery couriers, truck operators, and a wide range of other service providers. Our mission is to equip you with reliable and efficient tools that allow you to manage your schedules, meticulously track your earnings and expenses, and engage with a community that fosters connection and mutual support. In addition, we provide advanced safety and wellness features, including driving activity monitoring and the optional Focus Feature, which promotes well-being through collective rest coordination.

Our Privacy Policy complies with all applicable international, federal, and state data protection laws, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”) applicable within the European Union, the California Consumer Privacy Act (“CCPA”), and the specific privacy mandates of the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. By downloading, installing, or otherwise using the Gigways application, you expressly consent to the data collection and usage practices described in this policy. We strongly encourage you to read this document thoroughly to understand how your information is handled and protected by our systems.

2. Information We Collect

In our commitment to data minimization and privacy, we collect only the information that is necessary to provide, maintain, and improve our services. The categories of data we gather include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Personal Information: This category encompasses information that personally identifies you, such as your full name, email address, telephone number, and your state of residence. Additionally, we may collect any further profile information that you voluntarily provide in order to personalize your experience and facilitate app functionalities.
  • Activity and Usage Data: We collect detailed information about your work-related activities and app interactions. This includes, but is not limited to, data on your work schedules, hours logged, earnings and expenses tracked, and patterns of app usage. This information is instrumental in delivering seamless services and tailoring features to meet your needs.
  • Location and Motion Data (Optional and Consent-Based): With your explicit and informed consent, we may collect GPS location data and motion-related data to support various advanced features such as rest and break reminders, route optimization, and wellness initiatives tied to the Focus Feature. All such data collection is strictly opt-in, allowing you full control over whether or not to enable these capabilities.
  • Device and Technical Data: To maintain app security and ensure optimal performance, we collect technical details regarding your device, including device model, operating system version, IP address, and application logs. This data assists us in identifying and resolving technical issues, as well as optimizing the app for diverse device configurations.

3. Children’s Privacy

Protecting the privacy of minors is a responsibility we take seriously. The Gigways app and services are not designed for use by children under the age of 13 or the minimum age required by local laws. We do not knowingly solicit or collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a minor, we will take immediate steps to delete such information in accordance with legal obligations.

4. User Consent and Control

We obtain your explicit and informed consent through clear in-app prompts, settings toggles, and permission requests before collecting any sensitive data such as location and motion information. You can review, modify, or withdraw your consent at any time through the app’s settings or your device’s privacy controls without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing. Our commitment is to provide you with easy-to-understand controls to manage your privacy preferences.

5. How We Use Your Information

Your trust is essential, and we commit to using your information solely for legitimate and transparent purposes that directly benefit your user experience. Specifically, we employ your data to:

  • Facilitate and optimize the core functionalities of the Gigways app, including scheduling management, mileage tracking, logging expenses, and summarizing earnings, thereby empowering you to efficiently organize and monitor your gig economy work.
  • Support optional wellness and safety tools, including timely rest reminders and driving detection capabilities, activated exclusively with your consent. These features are designed to promote safer, healthier driving habits and overall well-being.
  • Create, maintain, and provide accurate work-related documentation such as detailed time logs and mileage reports, which can be utilized for personal record-keeping and tax preparation purposes.
  • Analyze anonymized, aggregated usage data to continually enhance the app’s functionality, interface design, and performance, ensuring that Gigways evolves in response to real user needs and behaviors.
  • Operate the optional Focus Feature, enabling users to voluntarily schedule rest days and wellness breaks. Data generated from participation supports calculation of driver density forecasts, providing valuable insights into driver activity and concentration by geographic location. Participation in this feature is strictly voluntary, and individual data is never shared with third parties.
  • We do not use your personal data for advertising, marketing, or promotional purposes, nor do we sell or distribute your information to third-party marketers or advertisers.

6. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

Gigways does not use any form of automated decision-making or profiling that would have a legal or significant effect on you. All decisions regarding your account and services are reviewed or implemented with appropriate human oversight.

7. Data Sharing and Security

The protection of your personal information is a cornerstone of our privacy commitment. Gigways does not engage in the sale, rental, or leasing of your personal data to third parties. However, to improve service delivery and app performance, we may share aggregated and anonymized data sets with carefully vetted service providers and trusted partners. This shared data is stripped of all personally identifiable information and is used solely for analytical or operational purposes.

We have implemented robust, industry-leading security measures—including advanced encryption protocols for data both in transit and at rest—to ensure your data is safeguarded against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Access to your personal information is strictly limited to Gigways employees, contractors, and service providers who require it to perform their job functions and who are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Specifically, we employ SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission, encrypt sensitive data at rest, conduct regular security audits, and apply best practices to maintain data integrity and confidentiality.

8. Third-Party Service Providers

To support our services, we engage trusted third-party providers including cloud hosting services, email communication platforms, and payment processors. These providers are contractually obligated to comply with applicable data protection laws and only process your data under our instructions. We carefully select these partners to maintain the highest standards of privacy and security.

9. Location and Tracking Compliance

Gigways fully complies with the privacy guidelines set forth by Apple and Google regarding location and motion tracking. All such features are disabled by default and are activated solely upon your explicit, informed consent. These features facilitate the provision of safety alerts, gentle rest reminders, accurate work tracking, and participation in the Focus Feature’s wellness initiatives. You maintain full control over enabling or disabling these features at any time through the app’s settings or your device’s privacy controls.

10. Driver Density Map Privacy

The Driver Density Map feature provides anonymized and aggregated location data exclusively from users who opt to share this information. The sole purpose of this feature is to present real-time insights into driver concentrations, helping users make informed decisions regarding work locations and schedules.

  • We do not disclose or share any individual’s precise location or personally identifiable information.
  • Participation is completely voluntary, and users may revoke location sharing at any time.
  • We maintain stringent security practices to ensure that all shared data is fully protected.

We may send notifications to keep you informed about important updates related to your work schedule, earnings, breaks, and safety reminders, including driving activity alerts—only after obtaining your explicit consent. You can customize or disable these notifications at any time through your device’s notification settings to suit your preferences.

13. Data Retention

Gigways retains personal and usage data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, including compliance with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing agreements. Approximate retention periods include:

  • Personal Information: Retained for the duration of your account activity plus 1 year after account deletion.
  • Activity and Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytical and service improvement purposes.
  • Location and Motion Data: Retained only during active app usage and for brief periods thereafter, unless otherwise consented.

Upon your request to delete your account or data, we will promptly delete all personally identifiable information except where retention is required by law for purposes such as tax reporting or legal compliance.

17. PV Rides Privacy, Safety, and Liability Notice

PV Rides is an optional feature designed solely to help users coordinate rides with individuals they personally know and trust. The feature functions only between users who have mutually added each other as friends or trusted contacts within the Gigways platform. PV Rides is not available to the general public, does not connect users with strangers, and is not intended for use with unknown or unverified individuals.

Private and Mutually Added Contacts

PV Rides may be used exclusively between users who have mutually confirmed each other as friends or trusted contacts. Both users must establish this connection before any PV Ride can be requested or accepted. Gigways does not match, assign, introduce, or connect users in any way and is not involved in initiating or arranging ride activities.

Not a Transportation Network Company

Gigways is not a Transportation Network Company (TNC), ride-hailing service, taxi service, dispatcher, commercial transportation provider, or mobility platform. Gigways does not provide transportation, does not operate vehicles, and does not control, supervise, or manage any activity that takes place between users who choose to coordinate private rides.

Optional Contributions Outside of Gigways

Any contribution offered or exchanged between users is entirely optional and takes place outside of Gigways. Gigways does not support, endorse, manage, process, track, or participate in any contribution or personal arrangement between users under any circumstances.

Information Shared for PV Rides

Only minimal information is used to facilitate coordination between mutually approved users, such as names and general trip request details. Gigways does not share precise GPS data or personal information with anyone outside the approved friend connection unless required by law or explicitly authorized by the user.

Consent for Phone Number Sharing

For the purpose of coordinating a PV Ride, both participating users may choose to share their phone numbers with one another. By requesting or accepting a PV Ride, users explicitly agree that:

  • Their phone number may be shared with the mutually approved contact for coordination purposes.
  • They may receive calls or messages only related to the privately arranged ride.
  • Gigways does not access, monitor, record, or store the content of calls or messages.

User Responsibility and Assumption of Risk

All PV Rides are voluntary, private arrangements between individuals who personally know and trust each other. Users acknowledge and accept full responsibility for their interactions and assume all risks associated with coordinating or participating in a PV Ride. Gigways does not verify the identity, behavior, driving ability, vehicle condition, safety practices, or reliability of any user.

No Duty of Oversight or Guarantees

Gigways has no duty to supervise, monitor, evaluate, or control any user activity related to PV Rides. Gigways provides the PV Rides feature “as is” without any warranties or guarantees regarding safety, suitability, legality, reliability, or fitness for a particular purpose.

Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Gigways LLC is not responsible or liable for any injuries, accidents, damages, losses, disputes, misconduct, or incidents that occur before, during, or after a privately coordinated PV Ride. All liability rests solely with the users who choose to participate.

Indemnification

Users agree to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Gigways LLC, its affiliates, employees, and representatives from any claims, damages, expenses, or liabilities arising out of their participation in PV Rides, their interactions with other users, or their violation of this Privacy Policy.

No Agency or Employment Relationship

Using PV Rides does not create any employment, agency, contractor, partnership, joint venture, or representative relationship between users and Gigways. Gigways does not direct or control user actions or decisions.

Opt-Out and User Control

Users may disable the PV Rides feature at any time through their account settings. Once disabled, all associated communication, visibility, and data use related to PV Rides will immediately stop.
