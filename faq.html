Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Gigways features, privacy, and driver tools.

1) What is Gigways? ▶

Gigways is a wellness and productivity app for gig drivers. Track miles, expenses, hours, and trips, plus use tools like Focus and Driver Density Forecasts.

2) Who can use Gigways? ▶

Any gig driver — rideshare (Uber, Lyft), delivery (DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub), shopping (Instacart), and more.

3) How does the Focus feature work? ▶

Focus lets you pick or join rest days. It shows popular rest times, sends reminders, and encourages wellness. 100% optional.

4) What is the Driver Density Forecast? ▶

A map showing real-time and forecasted driver concentrations (city, ZIP, or 5-mile grids) with Red = high, Green = low.

5) How is driver density calculated? ▶

We use anonymized, consented activity plus time-of-day population rules. All users see the same consistent estimates.

6) Does Gigways help with taxes? ▶

Yes — mileage and expense tracking is built in. We also partner with tax services for easy filing inside the app.

7) Is my data secure? ▶

Yes. We use encryption, strong security practices, and privacy-by-design. We never sell your personal information.

8) Can I control location and motion tracking? ▶

Yes. These features are off by default and only enabled with your consent. You can turn them off anytime.

9) Where is Gigways available? ▶

Currently in the United States.

10) Which devices are supported? ▶

iOS and Android smartphones running recent OS versions.

11) How accurate is mileage tracking? ▶

It’s designed for reliable capture with low battery drain. Keep permissions on and exclude Gigways from aggressive battery savers.

12) Can I export my data? ▶

Yes. You can export reports inside the app or request via support@gigways.com.

13) How do I delete my account or data? ▶

Request deletion in the app or by emailing support. We remove personal data unless law requires retention.

14) Will Gigways affect my gig platform accounts? ▶

No. Gigways is independent. Focus and Density are planning tools and do not connect to Uber, Lyft, or others.

15) I found a bug — how do I report it? ▶

Email support@gigways.com with details and screenshots. We’ll respond quickly.

16) Can teams or fleets use Gigways? ▶

Yes. Fleet and group tools are planned. Contact us if you want early access.

17) Does Gigways work offline? ▶

Basic tracking continues offline. Data syncs automatically once your phone reconnects to the internet.

18) How does Focus protect my privacy? ▶

Focus only shows aggregated rest trends. We never display individual driver choices or personal details.

19) How often is the Driver Density Forecast updated? ▶

Current activity updates frequently, and forecasts project 15-, 30-, and 60-minute windows ahead.

21) What are Gigways Rooftop Car Ads? ▶

Gigways Rooftop Ads allow drivers to place branded decals or rooftop toppers on their cars. This gives drivers an extra way to earn while companies get visible advertising on the road.

22) How much can drivers earn from car ads? ▶

Earnings vary by campaign and region. Drivers may receive a flat monthly rate or campaign-based payment. Gigways works to ensure fair compensation after covering printing and setup costs.

23) Do drivers install the rooftop ads themselves? ▶

Yes, most rooftop decals or toppers are simple to install. Gigways provides clear instructions and support. In some cases, local pickup or installation partners may be available.

24) What affiliate partners does Gigways work with? ▶

We partner with services that benefit drivers, such as tax filing (FreeTaxUSA), auto parts (AutoZone, NAPA), insurance (eHealth), and maintenance shops. These appear in-app as partner offers or discounts.

25) How do affiliate offers work inside the app? ▶

When you tap a partner link inside Gigways, you’re taken to their official site or WebView. If you complete a purchase or service, the partner pays Gigways a small commission, often with special discounts for you.
