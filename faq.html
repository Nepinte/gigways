Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Gigways features, privacy, and driver tools.

Drivers first Privacy by design Made for gig work
1) What is Gigways? ▶

Gigways is a wellness and productivity app for gig drivers. Track miles, expenses, hours, and trips, and use tools like Focus and Driver Density Forecasts.

2) Who can use Gigways? ▶

Any gig driver — rideshare (Uber, Lyft), delivery (DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub), shopping (Instacart), and more.

3) How does the Focus feature work? ▶

Focus lets you pick or join rest days. It shows popular rest times, sends reminders, and supports recovery. 100% optional.

4) What is the Driver Density Forecast? ▶

A map showing real-time and forecasted driver concentrations (by city, ZIP, or 5-mile grids) with Red = high, Green = low.

5) How is driver density calculated? ▶

We combine anonymized, consented activity with time-of-day & population rules. All users see consistent estimates. No personal locations are exposed.

6) Does Gigways help with taxes? ▶

Yes — mileage and expense tracking are built in. Inside the app, we also provide partner tax-filing options to make filing easier.

7) Is my data secure? ▶

Yes. We use encryption, strict security practices, and privacy-by-design. We never sell your personal information.

8) Can I control location and motion tracking? ▶

Yes. These are off by default and only enabled with your consent. You can disable permissions anytime via device or app settings.

9) Where is Gigways available? ▶

Currently in the United States.

10) Which devices are supported? ▶

iOS and Android smartphones running recent OS versions.

11) How accurate is mileage tracking? ▶

It’s tuned for reliable capture with low battery drain. Keep permissions granted and exclude Gigways from aggressive battery savers.

12) Can I export my data? ▶

Yes. Export mileage and expense reports in the app or request via support@gigways.com.

13) How do I delete my account or data? ▶

Request deletion in the app or by emailing support. We remove identifiable data unless law requires retention (e.g., tax compliance).

14) Will Gigways affect my gig platform accounts? ▶

No. Gigways is independent. Focus and Density are planning tools only and don’t connect to Uber, Lyft, etc.

15) I found a bug — how do I report it? ▶

Email steps, screenshots, and your device model to support@gigways.com. We’ll respond quickly.

16) Can teams or fleets use Gigways? ▶

Yes. Fleet and group tools are planned. Contact us if you want early access.

17) Does Gigways work offline? ▶

Basic tracking continues offline. Data syncs automatically once your phone reconnects to the internet.

18) How does Focus protect my privacy? ▶

Focus only shows aggregated rest trends. We never display an individual driver’s choices or personal details.

19) How often is the Driver Density Forecast updated? ▶

Current activity updates frequently, and forecasts project 15-, 30-, and 60-minute windows ahead.

21) What are Gigways Rooftop Car Ads? ▶

Gigways Rooftop Ads let drivers place branded decals or rooftop toppers on their cars. Drivers earn extra while brands get on-road visibility.

22) How much can drivers earn from car ads? ▶

Earnings vary by campaign and region. Compensation is typically a flat monthly rate or by campaign, designed to be fair after printing/setup costs.

23) Do drivers install the rooftop ads themselves? ▶

Most decals/toppers are simple to install with our instructions. In some locations, pickup or install partners may be available.

24) What affiliate partners does Gigways work with? ▶

Driver-focused partners like tax filing (e.g., FreeTaxUSA), auto parts (e.g., AutoZone, NAPA), insurance (e.g., eHealth), and maintenance shops. Offers appear in-app as partner perks/discounts.

25) How do affiliate offers work inside the app? ▶

Tapping a partner link opens their official site/WebView. If you complete a purchase or service, the partner pays a small commission to Gigways—often with a discount for you.
