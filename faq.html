Track mileage, expenses, work hours, and earnings so you can understand your real profit and drive smarter.

3Does Gigways connect to Uber/Lyft/DoorDash? ▶

No. Gigways is independent. You stay in control, and your gig platform accounts are not connected.

4How accurate is mileage tracking? ▶

Accuracy depends on device settings. Allow location access, and prevent battery optimization from restricting the app.

5Can I export mileage and expense reports? ▶

Yes. Export options depend on your app version. If you don’t see export, email support@gigways.com.

6Can Gigways help me understand real profit? ▶

Yes. Track miles, time, and expenses to see what you make after costs.

7What is Smart Scheduling? ▶

Set reminders for breaks and meals to stay consistent and reduce burnout.

8What is the Driver Density Map? ▶

It shows aggregated trends to help drivers plan smarter areas and reduce wasted time.

9What are Private Trips? ▶

Private Trips are rides with drivers you already know and trust — no public matching and no strangers.

10How do drivers manage Private Trips? ▶

Drivers can receive requests, coordinate details, and keep private trips organized.

11How do riders and drivers connect? ▶

Riders connect first. Drivers accept. Trips stay private between people who already know each other.

12Does Gigways set prices or handle payments for Private Trips? ▶

Gigways is a toolkit. Drivers are independent, and payment arrangements are handled between driver and rider.

13Do you sell driver data? ▶

No. We don’t sell your personal data.

14Can I control location permissions? ▶

Yes. You can enable/disable permissions anytime in your phone settings.

15How do I delete my account or data? ▶

Email support@gigways.com and we’ll guide you through the steps.

16Where is Gigways available? ▶

Check the App Store / Google Play listing for the latest supported regions.

17Which devices are supported? ▶

iOS and Android on recent OS versions. Keep your device updated for best results.

18I found a bug — how do I report it? ▶

Email support@gigways.com with steps and screenshots.

19What are Car Ads in Gigways? ▶

Opt-in ad campaigns where drivers can display approved ads and earn extra income, while businesses get local visibility.

20How much can drivers earn from car ads? ▶

Earnings vary by campaign, region, and requirements.

21Do I have to do car ads to use Gigways? ▶

No. Car ads are optional. Tracking and private trips work without ads.

22How can a business start a car ad campaign? ▶

Visit partners.html#forms and submit the campaign form.

23What types of businesses are a good fit for car ads? ▶

Local brands: restaurants, gyms, retail, services, events, and new openings.

24Do businesses get access to driver personal data? ▶

No. Campaigns run without sharing driver personal data.

25How do I contact support? ▶

Email support@gigways.com and include your device model and screenshots if possible.
