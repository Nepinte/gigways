About Us

Who We Are

GigWays is an all-in-one platform built to empower gig drivers—whether you drive rideshare, make deliveries, or run courier routes. Our mission is to help you take control of your schedule, reduce stress, and stay balanced while earning on the road. With GigWays, you can easily track miles, hours, expenses, and earnings in one place, keeping your work organized and your focus clear. Smart Scheduling tools offer gentle reminders for breaks and meals, promoting healthier routines and preventing burnout. We’ve also introduced Voice, a community-centered feature that lets drivers choose their rest days, ease fatigue, and see how many others are resting too. It’s designed to promote well-being, encourage healthy routines, and strengthen unity among drivers—all in a supportive and transparent way. Our Driver Density tool provides real-time insights into driver hotspots, helping you work smarter and boost your income. At GigWays, your privacy and independence come first. We never partner with ride-hailing or gig platforms, and your data is protected with strong encryption. Upcoming features are built just for you—like car ad rewards, tax filing tools, a job board, and an accident emergency fund and more.

Created by drivers, for drivers, GigWays is your trusted co-pilot—here to support, protect, and empower you on every trip.
