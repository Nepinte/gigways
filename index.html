FAQ

Is Gigways a rideshare or delivery company?

No. Gigways is a driver tools app. We don’t operate transportation or delivery services.

What can I track with Gigways?

Mileage, expenses, hours, and earnings — so you can understand your real profit.

What are Private Trips?

Coordinate rides or drop-offs with people you already know and trust — no public matching.

What is the Driver Density Map?

Aggregated, anonymized activity trends to help you plan smarter and reduce wasted time.

Do you sell driver data?

No. Privacy and independence are core to Gigways.

How do Car Ad Campaigns work for businesses?

Businesses can run local car advertising campaigns while supporting independent drivers.
