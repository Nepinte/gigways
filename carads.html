FAQs

How does pricing work?

Pricing is based on campaign duration, number of vehicles, market coverage, and the ad format you choose. Estimates shown are preliminary. Final pricing is confirmed after your campaign brief and rollout details are reviewed.

How do you ensure driver reliability and brand safety?

All drivers are opt-in and managed through our app-based system. Drivers must complete required tasks, follow placement guidelines, and submit proof throughout the campaign. Non-compliant drivers are removed.

Do you provide proof or reporting?

Yes. Drivers use our app to submit installation photos, weekly photo check-ins, and basic activity confirmations until the campaign ends. Mileage and task completion are tracked.

What kind of tracking is included?

Our system tracks driver participation, mileage ranges, and weekly task completion. For advertiser performance, we recommend adding a QR code, custom landing page, promo code, or call tracking number.
